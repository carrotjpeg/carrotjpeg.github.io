01. MIT Beaverworks Brailliance

Brailliance is an affordable braille display. Translating text to ASCII, and converting ASCII to binary with an ASIC, the chip's data sends signals to actuators that move the braille pins, ultimately reducing the price of a portable braille reader by 90%, aiding visually-impaired people in their daily life.
